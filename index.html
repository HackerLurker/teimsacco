WELCOME TO TEIM SACCO

Together, we build financial security and long-term prosperity.

TEIM (Technicians, Engineers, Installers & Mechanics) SACCO is a financial cooperative created by professionals in the technical field, for professionals in the technical field. We bank with the Cooperative Bank of Kenya.

OUR OBJECTIVE

We understand the challenges faced by skilled workers — irregular income cycles, equipment financing needs, school fees demands, and business growth capital. TEIM exists to provide structured savings, accessible credit, and professional empowerment tailored specifically for our members.
