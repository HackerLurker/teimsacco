WHO ARE WE ?

Founded in 2019, TEIM SACCO is a registered cooperative society uniting Technicians, Engineers, Installers, and Mechanics across Kenya. We empower members through savings, affordable loans, and professional development, fostering financial growth and strengthening the community of skilled professionals driving Kenya’s progress.

We exist to provide affordable financial services and promote professional advancement within the technical industry.

OUR VISION

To be the leading SACCO empowering technical professionals in Kenya.

We aim to provide accessible financial services, professional development opportunities, and economic empowerment to technicians and skilled professionals through cooperative principles.
